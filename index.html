Our Story

Anand Christian Seva Sansthan, Non-Profit Organization

Anand Christian Seva Sansthan is a charitable organization based in Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, India. we are dedicated to serving the community and addressing key social issues such as children's welfare, disaster management, access to clean drinking water, education, literacy, and health care. We operates with a mission to uplift underprivileged communities through various support programs and initiatives. It provides educational resources, health services, and emergency relief to those in need. The organization is known for its active engagement in community development and its commitment to improving the quality of life for the marginalized sections of society. Anand Christian Seva Sansthan’s efforts reflect its foundational values of compassion, service, and social responsibility, making a significant impact on the lives of many individuals in different regions.

Our Mission

Anand Christian Seva Sansthan aims to contribute to the well-being of humanity by addressing key social issues such as children's welfare, disaster management, drinking water, education & literacy, and health. The organization is dedicated to advancing the progress of the nation and promoting global harmony through its charitable activities and community services

Our Vision

The vision of Anand Christian Seva Sansthan is to cultivate inner values and foster a sense of global unity. We strive to create a harmonious society where individuals can achieve their full potential, contributing to a peaceful and prosperous Nation.

Rakesh Masih

Founder/ Director

Mr. Rakesh Masih is the founder of Anand Christian Seva Sansthan, an organization based in Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, India. The organization is dedicated to serving the community and providing social, Educational, and charitable services. Mr. Masih holds a prominent role within the organization and is actively involved in its operations and outreach activities. His leadership has been pivotal in the development and execution of various programs aimed at uplifting and supporting the community. Mr. Masih's main aim is to provide help of all kinds which is required to a needy person to make sure that he may become something.
